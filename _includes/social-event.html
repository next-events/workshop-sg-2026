29 Apr 2026: Dinner

We will have dinner at Yummy Palace, a beloved dim sum and Cantonese restaurant tucked away in the industrial enclave of Defu Lane. Known for its generous portions, authentic flavours, and lively atmosphere, Yummy Palace has long been a local favourite for group gatherings. Expect classic steamed and baked dim sum, roast meats, and hearty wok-fried dishes.

1 May 2026: Half-day Tour

We will spend the morning at Rainforest Wild ASIA (20 Mandai Lake Road, Singapore 729825), Asia's first wildlife adventure park opened in March 2025. Spanning 13 hectares of lush greenery, the park features eight immersive zones home to 36 species including the Malayan tiger, sun bear, François' langur and the Philippine spotted deer. Explore the elevated canopy walkway for sweeping rainforest views, or take the Orange Trek across logs and boulders for a closer encounter with nature.
